NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier professional basketball league in the United States and Canada. Founded in 1946 as the Basketball Association of America (BAA), it adopted its current name in 1949 after merging with the National Basketball League (NBL).

Outstanding Players

In every sport there are legends that left a great mark in the passing of time, and of course the most important basketball league has its references that are milestones in the NBA itself.

Michael Jordan

Considered by many to be the greatest basketball player of all time, Jordan won six NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls and was named Finals MVP on all of those occasions

Go

LeBron James

A versatile player who has won championships with three different teams (Miami Heat, Cleveland Cavaliers and Los Angeles Lakers) and is the NBA's all-time leader in points scored.

Kobe Bryant

He won five championships with the Los Angeles Lakers and is known for his competitive mentality and exceptional offensive skills

Bill Russell

With eleven championships in his career, he is the player with the most titles in NBA history. His defensive dominance and ability to win are legendary

Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

He held the record for most points scored in NBA history for many years until he was surpassed by LeBron James. He won six championships and was named MVP six times
